2023/2024 SEASON
-The Club played a promotion league under Uhlelo Sub LFA and played a knockout tournament that is played by all Mkhondo SUB LFAs ,and the team was eliminated on their second game ,the tournament is sponsored by TWK and it's called VUMBUKA TRUST .
-On the same season December 2023 the club played Mkhondo festive tournament which was open for all Mkhondo registered teams in all Sub LFAs and a TOURNAMENT sponsored by the ANC YOUTH LEAGUE in Ward 6 the club made it to the finals in both Tournaments. The final was played at the same day and time so the club was forced to split into 2 teams , unfortunately they didn't win on the Mkhondo festive tournament, and the prizes were a Soccer ball,Medals and R3000 Cash but fortunate enough the other team won in the TOURNAMENT that was sponsored by the ANC YOUTH LEAGUE in Ward 6 The prizes were Medals, Trophy and a Sheep..

The management of the club appreciated the perfomance of the club by arranging a celebration of appreciation at Crescent Park

-The club finished the Promotion league at number 5
